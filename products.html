N.P.K(6,6,40)

Fully water-soluble high potassium fertilizer produced from high quality materials, easily absorbed by plants promoting roots and leaves growth. Promotes high concentration of sugars in cells making fruits sweeter and crucial for high yielding crops. Provide plants with essential nutrients for plant health and growth. High potassium fertilizer enhances plant's ability to absorb nitrogen from the ground and produce proteins and enhance crop yields. Increases root growth and improve drought resistance. Recommended when soil levels or tissue samples show low potassium levels or during setting buds, maturation and blooming. Increase the validity of fruits and increase its storage ability. Used to improve quality of fruits (size-TSS-color) for improved export features.
